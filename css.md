## Flexbox

Components:

1 - Creation: display

2 - Direction: flex-flow (flex-direction, flex-wrap)

3 - Alignment: justify-content, align-items, align-self, align-content

4 - Ordering: order

5 - Flexibility: flex (flex-grow, flex-shrink, flex-basis)
